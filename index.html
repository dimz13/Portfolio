About Me

Hello there! I'm Dimple Shanbhag, budding full stack developer and lifelong learner. Finance and Telecom domains are where I have contributed. Started off my career in Testing and moved onto automation. From here I moved to frontend development. When i was given the opportunity to be part of a tools development team and given the front end responsibility I found my calling. Presenting a nice usable UI for even the most technically challenged person , across different browsers and screens, and elegant code with reusable components.....whats not to like. Its a thrill ride everytime. The constant changes and new frameworks that are available is a challenge to keep up with.

Outside of work and learning, I'm a podcast junkie. I listen to any an every podcast I can get my hands on. I'm a big fan of the Joe Rogan podcast.My commute times are my podcast listening time(Makes it so much more enjoyable) Reading fiction and non-fiction is also a major past time. I have memberships in multiple county libraries and use Libby to borrow both audio and ebooks. Free access to libraries is one of the things I love about this country. In many other countries you have to have a paid membership to borrow books.What can I say I love libraries..
